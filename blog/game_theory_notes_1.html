Revenue Equivalence Theorem

Statement

Any auction mechanism satisfying:

  1. i.i.d. valuations
  2. Symmetric BNE with strictly increasing differentiable strategies
  3. Zero expected payment for θi=0\theta_i = 0θi​=0
